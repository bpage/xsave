Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2025  ·  Governed by the laws of California, USA

The short version

We don't collect your personal information, we don't store videos on our servers, and we don't sell data to anyone. The only third-party service that may track you is Google AdSense, which serves ads to keep the site free.

What we do not collect

XSAVE does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information. We don't ask you to create an account. We don't log the URLs you submit beyond what a standard web server keeps temporarily in memory to process your request. We don't store those URLs after your session ends.

Video content

When you submit a Twitter / X URL, our server contacts the platform to extract a direct stream link and returns it to your browser. We do not download, cache, or store any video files. All video data goes directly from Twitter / X's servers to your device.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display ads on the homepage. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you personalized ads based on your browsing history across sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at adssettings.google.com, or install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. For more, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics in the future to understand aggregate traffic patterns (pages visited, device types, general geography). If enabled, this data is anonymous and does not identify individual users. We will update this policy when Analytics is active.

Cookies

XSAVE itself does not set any cookies. Any cookies present in your browser while using the site come from Google AdSense or other third-party services loaded on the page.

Third-party links

This site links to Twitter / X and may reference other external services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Once you follow a link off XSAVE, their policies apply.

Children

XSAVE is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of the page. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at info@xsave.pics.
